About FeynML

FeynML was created as an academic and engineering initiative to make machine learning systems easier to understand, diagnose, and improve.

The project was developed by **Arman Kaushik**, a Computer Science undergraduate at Swami Keshvanand Institute of Technology (SKIT), Jaipur.

FeynML combines statistical diagnostics, model observability, root-cause analysis, and automated reporting into a single investigation platform.

Mission

To make machine learning reliability accessible, explainable, and practical for everyone—from students learning ML concepts to professionals deploying models in production.

The Architecture

Phase 1: Diagnostic Core

Stateless engines for calibration, fairness, and slice performance discovery.

Phase 2: Observability

Drift detection monitoring the shift between training and production distributions.

Phase 3: Root Cause

Data integrity scanners for target leakage, label noise, and missing data mechanisms.
