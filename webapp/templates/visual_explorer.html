How to Use Visual Explorer

Build, explore, and export custom charts from your dataset.

📐

Dimensions

Categorical columns like mainroad, furnishingstatus. Use on X Axis for grouping. Click chip to auto-fill X Axis field.

📏

Measures

Numeric columns like price, area. Use on Y Axis for values. Click chip to auto-fill Y Axis field.

📊

Chart Types

Scatter — relationship between two numbers
Bar — compare categories
Histogram — distribution of one column
Box Plot — spread and outliers
Line — trend over sorted X
Heatmap — correlation matrix
Violin — distribution shape by category

🎨

Color By

Split chart by a third column using color. Best used with a Dimension (categorical column). Creates automatic legend per unique value.

🏷️

Legend

Auto-appears when Color By is set. Click legend items to show/hide specific groups. Toggle Show Legend off for cleaner single-color charts.

🔍

Filters

Narrow your data before plotting. Example: bedrooms > 2 shows only houses with more than 2 bedrooms. Add multiple filters to combine conditions.

🛠️

Chart Toolbar

Zoom — drag to zoom into any region
Pan — move around zoomed chart
Reset Axes — return to full view
Download PNG — save full resolution image

💡

Suggested Charts

Auto-recommended charts based on your dataset's drift results and noise findings. One click to generate without manual axis selection.
